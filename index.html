Visualizing Prescription Painkiller Deaths

America is currently gripped in an opioid epidemic, drug overdoses are now the leading cause of death among Americans under 50. Big numbers are hard to visualize, this website aims to overcome that.

It should be noted this data only concerns prescription opioid deaths - heroin or other drug deaths are not included.
